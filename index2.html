I am a post-doct at IMECC, I work with Marcos JARDIM.

In May 2023, I defended my PhD thesis on the subject “Stable equivariant sheaves on toric varieties” at the Laboratoire de Mathématiques de Bretagne Occidentale (LMBA) under the supervision of Carl TIPLER.

My research fields are algebraic geometry and complex geometry. Currently, I am interested in the following:
  • Study of stability of reflexive sheaves
  • Prescription of singularities on reflexive sheaves
  • Existence of Ulrich sheaves
  • Bridgeland stability and Z-critical connections
